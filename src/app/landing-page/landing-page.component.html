Influences:

Murder Bizkits are influenced by many elements old and new from tradition hardcore punk like Dead Kennedys and FEAR to Crossover Trash right upto modern day energetic Psychedelic Rock acts like Bo Ningen to Wormrot and Melt Banana all influences are ongoing.
